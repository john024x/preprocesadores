Sorting hat

The Sorting Hat is one of the cleverest enchanted objects most witches and wizards will ever meet. It literally contains the intelligence of the four founders, can speak (through a rip near its brim) and is skilled at Legilimency, which enables it to look into the wearer’s head and divine his or her capabilities or mood.The famous Hogwarts Sorting Hat gives an account of its own genesis in a series of songs sung at the beginning of each school year.
